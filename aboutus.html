About The Gallery Café

The Gallery Café, nestled in the heart of Colombo, is more than just a restaurant; it's a place where art meets gastronomy. Established in 2010, our café has been dedicated to providing a unique dining experience that blends exquisite cuisine with a vibrant, artistic atmosphere. Our philosophy revolves around the idea that dining should be an immersive experience, where every detail, from the decor to the dishes, tells a story.

Our menu is a celebration of flavors from around the world, with a particular focus on Sri Lankan, Chinese, and Italian cuisines. We take pride in sourcing the freshest ingredients and crafting each dish with care and creativity. Whether you're here for a casual lunch, a romantic dinner, or a special celebration, The Gallery Café offers something for everyone.

The ambiance of The Gallery Café is designed to be a feast for the senses. The walls are adorned with art from local and international artists, creating a gallery-like setting that inspires and intrigues. Our outdoor seating area provides a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of the city, making it the perfect spot to relax and unwind.

At The Gallery Café, we believe in the power of community. We regularly host special events, art exhibitions, and live performances, bringing together people from all walks of life to share in the joy of food, art, and culture. We are also committed to sustainability and giving back to the community, through various initiatives and partnerships with local organizations.

We invite you to join us at The Gallery Café and experience the perfect blend of art, culture, and culinary excellence. Our team is dedicated to ensuring that every visit is a memorable one, filled with delicious food, great conversation, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere.
